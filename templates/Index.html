US Delayed Flights

Predict if your flight will be delayed

The data visualized in this site originally came from the Kaggle website originally sourced from the Bureau of Transportation Statistics, Govt. of the USA. This data has over 500k flights in January 2019 and over 600k flights in January 2020. The data has a delay indicator for arrival and departure. The departure indicator tells you if the flight departed late by 15 minutes or more. The arrival indicator tells you if the flight arrived late by 15 minutes or more. There are also indicators for Cancelled and Diverted flights. Cancelled/Diverted flights do not have an arrival delay indicator and were excluded from charts showing arrival delay details.
