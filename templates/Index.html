US Delayed Flights

Predict if your flight will be delayed

The data for this site originally came from the Kaggle website which has a cleaned-up version of the Bureau of Transportation Statistics, Govt. of the USA dataset. It contains delayed flight data for the months of January in 2019 and 2020. The data has information on over 580 thousand US flights per year. The Delay indicators in the dataset indicate delay of 15 minutes or more. There is a delay indicator for both departure and arrival. When reporting percent delays, we excluded where these delay indicators were blank which is the case for the cancelled and diverted flights. Airport charts are also limited to the top 30 busiest airports.
