US Delayed Flights

Predict if your flight will be delayed

The data originally came from the Kaggle website which has a concatenated and cleaned-up version of the Bureau of Transportation Statistics, Govt. of the USA dataset. It contains delayed flight data for the months of January in 2019 and 2020. The data has over 550 thousand rows per year. We limited our analysis to include everything but the null values in the dataset.
